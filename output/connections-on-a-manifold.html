Connections on a manifold

Posted on April 22, 2020 in maths

We've talked briefly about smooth manifolds in previous post. The need to do calculus on the manifold was the motivation for us to limit our considerations to manifolds with smooth structure. In this post we are going to talk about the approach to defining a derivative on a manifold.

Differentiating a function on a manifold is straight forward. We have a smooth atlas, from which we can pick a chart and go to \(\mathbb R^n\) where usual definitions of derivatives from analysis work just fine.
